About Me

Here is an introduction about my life in Tongji

Hi, welcome to visit my personal pages. I am now a senior student major in software engineering in Tongji University.

However, when I was a freshman, I chose to major in mechanical and electronic. But not for long, I found I was interested in computer science and software development. Afterwards, I switched to Software Engineering, and that’s why I need to take one more year of basic courses after my freshman year. But I never regretted it, due to my great thirst of absorbing knowledge from what I’m interested in.

So that's why almost everything you can see in the correlated with programming. But coding is not my whole life, I also have other interests. I like design because I think it is the basis of solving problems, and it does exist everywhere in our life. I like to contact with users, listening to their requirements and advice, sharing our creativity to come up with a better solution. I like to involve in multi-disciplinary project, cooperating with people from different fields, to see the miraculous spark from the collision of different ideas and thinking modes. Human-Computer Interaction, which contains all of my interests, would undoubtedly be my best major.
